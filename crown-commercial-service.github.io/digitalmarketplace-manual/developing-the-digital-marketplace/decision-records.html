Recording decisions on the Digital Marketplace¶

There is a team drive folder to keep a record of important decisions, and the context in which they were made.

In the folder there is also a README document which explains the role of the Decisions Log, Requests for Comment (RFCs), Architectural Decision Records (ADRs), Decision Record Document (DRDs)
